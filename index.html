Mechanical engineering

What is mechanical engineering?

Mechanical engineering first started sometime around the industrial revolution in the 1800s in Europe. Today, mechanical engineering is still an important competitive job that many people want to be apart of. This type of engineering is a branch of engineering that works on external and internal machines like:

  • Cars
  • Car engines
  • Turbines
  • And even satellites

Hardware in mechanical engineering

In the early 2000s, the modern 3d printer was made. 3d printers can create almost any thing you can imagine out of things like plastic to things like metal. Today, some people use a 3d printer to print out new replacement parts for things like broken clocks.

Software in mechanical engineerings

If you are to use an 3d printer, you would also need 3d printing software to code what the thing you are trying build with the printer. 3d printing softwares are needed apps that create a digital 3d object for the 3d printer to understand. Without any 3d printing software, your 3d printer wouldn't know what to print.

The future of mechanical engineering

An example I believe could happen is by telling an AI to make something using a verbal command to make something like a car engine. Hopefully, it would just 3d print the object within a hour.
