Mechanical engineering

What is mechanical engineering?

Mechanical engineering is a branch of engineering that works on machines like:

  • Cars
  • Car engines
  • Turbines
  • Or even satellites

Hardware in mechanical engineering

In the early 2000s, the modern 3d printer was made. 3d printers can create almost any thing you can imagine out of things like plastic of metal. Today, some people use a 3d printer to print out new replacement parts for things like broken clocks.

Software in mechanical engineerings

If you are to use an 3d printer, you would also need 3d printing software to code what the thing you are trying build with the printer. 3d printing softwares are needed apps that create a digital 3d object for the 3d printer to understand. Without any 3d printing software, your 3d printer wouldn't know what to print.

The future of mechanical engineering

In the future, we may be able to get artificial intelligence to create objects just by saying what we want made. An example I believe could happen is telling an AI to make a car engine. Hopefully, it would just 3d print an engine using a simple line of text.
